Spent the morning studying the Asimov Collective portfolio for the third time. The restraint is the design. No motion libraries, no canvas effects — just type, image, and whitespace doing all the work. Pre-rendered video where others would reach for WebGL.

Reading Pallasmaa's *Eyes of the Skin*, the chapter on peripheral vision. He argues that focused vision makes us spectators; peripheral vision draws us into participation. Thinking about what that means for scroll behavior.

Sketched out the type scale. Minor third ratio, Futura PT for headings, Cormorant Garamond for body. The contrast between geometric precision and calligraphic warmth feels right — it mirrors the architecture-software tension.
